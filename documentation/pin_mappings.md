# Pin Mappings

## Traffic Light Control Pins

| Direction | Red  | Yellow | Green |
|-----------|------|--------|-------|
| North     | D2   | D3     | D4    |
| South     | D5   | D6     | D7    |
| East      | D8   | D9     | D10   |
